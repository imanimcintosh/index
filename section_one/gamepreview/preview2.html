The screen is a surface, one that is so visible yet so discreet. It masters the art of camoflauge making it easy for us to move along our virtual lives.

But, do you really know your screen? What does it mean to be psycially intimate with a virtual space? What does sliding your finger across this plane feel like ? Why do your fingers move the way they do when interacting with the screen?

These exercises will explore some of the ways we interact with our phones, from within the screen and beyond. As you move along the excerises, I encourage you to think about and be aware of the physicality of the screen, the phone and your sense of movement.
