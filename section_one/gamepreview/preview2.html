The screen is a surface, one that is so visible yet so discreet. It masters the art of camoflauge making it easy for us to move along our virtual lives.

But, do you really know your screen? What does sliding your finger across this plane feel like ? Why do your fingers move the way they do when interacting with the screen?

As you move along the excerises, I encourage you to think about and be aware of the physicality of the screen and your sense of movement.
